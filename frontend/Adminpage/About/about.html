Our Story

AUB Tutor Hub was born from a simple yet powerful observation: the best tutors are often fellow students who have recently conquered the same academic challenges you're facing.

As AUB students ourselves, we experienced firsthand the struggle of finding quality, affordable tutoring that truly understood the unique demands of our university's rigorous academic environment.

What started as a small group of friends helping each other study has evolved into AUB's premier peer tutoring platform, connecting thousands of students with expert tutors who speak their academic language.

Today, we're proud to be the bridge that connects academic excellence with accessibility, ensuring every AUB student has the support they need to thrive.

How We Started

The journey from a simple idea to AUB's leading tutoring platform

The Spark - Fall 2022

Three AUB students struggling with calculus couldn't find affordable tutoring. They decided to help each other and discovered the power of peer learning.

Growing the Circle - Spring 2023

Word spread quickly. What started as 3 students became 30, then 100. The informal study groups were working, but organization was needed.

Building the Platform - Summer 2023

Using skills learned in computer science classes, the founders built the first version of AUB Tutor Hub during summer break.

Official Launch - Fall 2023

AUB Tutor Hub officially launched with 50 verified tutors across 15 subjects. The response was overwhelming and immediate.

Today - 2024

Now serving thousands of students with hundreds of expert tutors, we continue to innovate and expand our impact across the AUB community.
